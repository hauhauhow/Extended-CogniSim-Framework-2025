# Sprint Review Feedback: Final Test Plan for Sprint 1

## General Feedback
Firstly, I want to commend the team on the comprehensive nature of the Test Plan. The attention to detail, the inclusion of industry-standard frameworks, and the clear delineation of roles and responsibilities are all indicative of a well-thought-out approach to Digital Operational Resilience (DOR). However, as we move forward, I would like to offer some specific feedback to ensure that our test plan is not only thorough but also actionable and aligned with our organizational goals.

## Feedback on Implementation Requirements

### Execution Procedures
- The establishment of a DOR Committee and the development of a DOR Testing Policy are excellent starting points. However, it would be beneficial to include a timeline for the formation of the committee and the completion of the policy to ensure accountability.
- When identifying critical ICT systems and assets, consider involving stakeholders from different departments to ensure a holistic view of the organization's risk profile.
- For test scenarios, it would be advantageous to include examples or templates to guide the team in their design process.

### Tools and Methodologies
- The selection of tools is appropriate, but we should also consider the integration of these tools into our existing systems to ensure smooth operation.
- It is important to ensure that the tools selected are not only industry-standard but also compatible with each other to avoid any interoperability issues.
- The adoption of the MITRE ATT&CK framework is commendable. However, we should also plan for training or workshops to ensure that the team is proficient in using this framework.

### Testing Frequencies and Schedules
- The testing frequencies are well-defined. However, we should also consider the impact of testing on business operations and plan for testing during off-peak hours if necessary.
- The plan should include a contingency schedule for delayed tests or unforeseen circumstances that could affect the testing timeline.

### Resource Allocation and Role Definitions
- While roles are defined, it would be beneficial to include a RACI matrix to clarify the responsibilities, accountability, and communication lines among the team members.
- Training is mentioned, but we need to specify the frequency and content of the training sessions to ensure they are relevant and up-to-date.

### Measurable KPIs and Success Criteria
- The KPIs and success criteria are well-defined. However, we should also establish a benchmark or target for each KPI to measure our progress effectively.
- Consider adding a feedback loop from stakeholders on the effectiveness of the remediation actions taken.

### Documentation and Audit Trail Processes
- The documentation process is thorough, but we should also plan for regular audits of the documentation to ensure its accuracy and completeness.
- Implementing version control is critical, but we should also specify the tools or systems we will use for this purpose.
