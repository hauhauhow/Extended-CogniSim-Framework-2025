# DORA Compliance Test Plan for Article 24 - Large Bank

## 1. Introduction
This document outlines a test plan for assessing the compliance of a large bank with the requirements of Article 24 of the Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA). The purpose of this test plan is to ensure that the bank's operational and security controls are in line with the regulatory directives set forth in Article 24.

## 2. Scope
The scope of this test plan includes assessing the bank's operational resilience, incident detection and response capabilities, risk management processes, and overall readiness to address disruptive events as mandated by Article 24 of DORA. The assessment will focus on the bank's ability to identify, respond to, and recover from operational disruptions.

## 3. Test Objectives
- Evaluate the bank's operational resilience framework and controls.
- Assess the bank's incident detection and response procedures.
- Verify the effectiveness of the bank's risk management processes in addressing operational disruptions.
- Ensure that the bank has appropriate measures in place to prevent and mitigate operational failures.

## 4. Test Methodology
The compliance test will involve a combination of document reviews, interviews with key stakeholders, and technical assessments. The following methodologies will be employed:
- Review of operational resilience policies, procedures, and documentation.
- Interviews with key personnel responsible for operational resilience, incident response, and risk management.
- Technical assessments of the bank's IT infrastructure, security controls, and incident detection systems.

## 5. Test Activities
1. Review of the bank's operational resilience policies and procedures.
2. Assessment of the bank's incident detection and response capabilities.
3. Evaluation of the bank's risk management processes.
4. Technical assessment of the bank's IT infrastructure and security controls.
5. Validation of the bank's business continuity and disaster recovery plans.
6. Identification of gaps and recommendations for improvement.

## 6. Test Deliverables
- Compliance assessment report outlining the findings, gaps, and recommendations.
- Action plan detailing remediation steps for addressing identified gaps.
- Executive summary highlighting key findings and recommendations for senior management.

## 8. Test Team
The test team will consist of experienced compliance professionals with expertise in operational resilience, risk management, and information security. The team will be led by a designated project manager who will oversee the test activities and ensure timely completion of the assessment.

## 9. Conclusion
This compliance test plan aims to assist the large bank in assessing its compliance with the requirements of Article 24 of DORA and identifying areas for improvement in its operational resilience and incident response capabilities. By conducting a thorough assessment and providing actionable recommendations, the bank can enhance its readiness to address operational disruptions and maintain regulatory compliance.
