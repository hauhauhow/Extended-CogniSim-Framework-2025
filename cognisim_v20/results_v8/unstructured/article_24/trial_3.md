# DORA Compliance Test Plan for Article 24 - Large Bank

## Objective:
The objective of this test plan is to ensure that the large bank is compliant with the requirements of Article 24 of the Data Ownership and Rights Act (DORA) related to data protection and security.

## Scope:
The test plan will focus on evaluating the bank's practices, policies, and procedures related to data protection and security as outlined in Article 24 of DORA. This includes assessing the bank's data governance framework, data security measures, data breach response procedures, and compliance with data protection regulations.

## Test Activities:
1. Review of Policies and Procedures:
   - Review the bank's data protection policies and procedures to ensure they align with the requirements of Article 24 of DORA.
   - Verify that the policies cover data classification, access controls, encryption, data retention, and data sharing.

2. Data Governance Framework:
   - Evaluate the bank's data governance framework to ensure that it includes mechanisms for data ownership, accountability, and oversight.
   - Check if the bank has designated a Data Protection Officer (DPO) responsible for monitoring compliance with data protection regulations.

3. Data Security Measures:
   - Assess the bank's data security measures including encryption protocols, access controls, and network security.
   - Conduct vulnerability assessments and penetration testing to identify and address any security weaknesses.

4. Data Breach Response Procedures:
   - Review the bank's data breach response procedures to ensure they are in line with legal requirements.
   - Test the incident response plan through simulated data breach scenarios to assess the bank's readiness and effectiveness in responding to breaches.

5. Compliance with Data Protection Regulations:
   - Verify that the bank is compliant with other relevant data protection regulations such as GDPR, CCPA, etc.
   - Check if the bank has mechanisms in place for obtaining user consent for data processing activities.

6. Reporting and Documentation:
   - Ensure that the bank maintains accurate records of data processing activities, data breaches, and compliance assessments.
   - Verify that the bank has mechanisms for reporting data breaches to regulatory authorities and affected individuals.

## Test Execution:
1. Conduct interviews with key stakeholders involved in data protection and security.
2. Perform document reviews of policies, procedures, and compliance documentation.
3. Conduct technical assessments of data security measures and controls.
4. Execute simulated data breach scenarios to test response procedures.
5. Compile findings and recommendations for remediation.

## Deliverables:
1. Test plan outlining the scope, objectives, and activities.
2. Test results report detailing findings, recommendations, and compliance status.
3. Remediation plan with actionable steps for addressing any identified gaps.
