Title: DORA Compliance Test Plan for Article 24 - Large Bank

1. Introduction:
   - Overview of the purpose of the test plan
   - Explanation of Article 24 requirements under the Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA)
   - Importance of compliance for a large bank
   - Scope and objectives of the compliance test plan

2. Test Environment:
   - Description of the testing environment
   - Tools and resources required for testing
   - Identification of systems and processes to be tested

3. Compliance Testing Procedures:
   - Review and assessment of the bank's IT systems and services
   - Evaluation of the bank's operational and security controls
   - Testing of incident response and reporting procedures
   - Verification of data protection and recovery measures

4. Testing Scenarios:
   - Creation of test scenarios based on Article 24 requirements
   - Simulation of cyber threats and incidents
   - Testing of system recovery and continuity measures
   - Evaluation of data breach response procedures

5. Testing Schedule:
   - Allocation of timeframes for each testing phase
   - Coordination with relevant stakeholders and teams
   - Definition of testing milestones and deliverables

6. Reporting and Documentation:
   - Compilation of test results and findings
   - Documentation of compliance gaps and recommendations
   - Preparation of a detailed test report for management and regulatory purposes

7. Remediation Plan:
   - Development of a remediation plan for addressing compliance issues
   - Assignment of responsibilities for implementing corrective actions
   - Follow-up testing to verify the effectiveness of remediation efforts

8. Compliance Validation:
   - Validation of compliance with Article 24 requirements
   - Confirmation of the bank's readiness for regulatory audits
   - Submission of compliance reports to regulatory authorities

9. Conclusion:
   - Summary of the compliance test plan
   - Acknowledgment of key stakeholders and team members
   - Next steps for ongoing compliance monitoring and testing

10. Appendices:
    - Supporting documentation, test scripts, and checklists
    - Contact information for key personnel involved in the compliance testing process

This DORA compliance test plan for Article 24 is designed to ensure that the large bank meets regulatory requirements and maintains operational resilience in the face of cyber threats and disruptions. It should be carried out with thoroughness and attention to detail to safeguard the bank's systems and data.
